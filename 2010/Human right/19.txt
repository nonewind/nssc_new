Human Rights Commissioner on new media law in Hungary - Federal Foreign Office 
    22.12.2010 - Press release

“Independence of the media and a diverse media landscape are core values of the European Union. Competition of ideas and public debate are hallmarks of a free and democratic society. Free media is something we Europeans stand up for all over the world. This means that governments and parliaments in all EU member states must clearly commit to and actively protect freedom of the media. As the future EU Presidency, Hungary bears a special responsibility here.”
