Press conference with outgoing Human Rights Commissioner Günter Nooke - Federal Foreign Office 
    22.03.2010 - Press release
Günter Nooke is leaving his post after four years as Federal Government Commissioner for Human Rights Policy and Humanitarian Aid at the Federal Foreign Office.
In this connection he will give a press conference at the Federal Foreign Office on Thursday (25 March) at 11.30 a.m., at which he will take stock, report on his experiences and identify the challenges that lie ahead.
