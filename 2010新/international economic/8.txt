Federal Minister Westerwelle to meet Italian Foreign Minister Frattini - Federal Foreign Office 
    11.01.2011 - Press release
On Wednesday, 12 January, Federal Foreign Minister Guido Westerwelle is to meet Italian Foreign Minister Franco Frattini at the Federal Foreign Office as part of the German-Italian intergovernmental consultations.
The Ministers will discuss current issues on the European and international agendas, as well as the economic momentum in the euro area.
