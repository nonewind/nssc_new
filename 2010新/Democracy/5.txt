Federal Minister Westerwelle on the Iraq elections - Federal Foreign Office 
    07.03.2010 - Press release
Today (7 March) in Buenos Aires Federal Foreign Minister Guido Westerwelle had this to say regarding the elections in Iraq:
”I have the utmost respect for the many courageous women and men who declared their support for democracy by participating in the elections.
At the same time I condemn the attacks that cast a shadow over Iraq’s election day.“
