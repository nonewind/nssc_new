Westerwelle condemns Moscow bomb attacks - Federal Foreign Office 
    29.03.2010 - Press release
Before departing Berlin for Canada, Federal Foreign Minister Guido Westerwelle today (29 March) made the following statement on the bomb attacks on two Moscow metro stations:
“We most strongly condemn the cowardly attacks in Moscow. These attacks are abominable and can in no way be justified. We are filled with sorrow, and we extend our sympathy to the families of the victims. For the time being, I assume that there are no Germans among the victims.”
