Cooperation for the Future: EU-Latin America/Caribbean Forum on Energy Policy - Federal Foreign Office 
    27.04.2010 - Press release
This Thursday (29 April), Federal Minister for Foreign Affairs Guido Westerwelle and Federal Minister for Economic Cooperation and Development Dirk Niebel will jointly open the two-day EU-Latin America/Caribbean (EU-LAC) Forum on Technological Cooperation, Renewable Energies and Energy Efficiency at the Federal Foreign Office. During those two days political, business, academic and civil-society experts from over 60 EU, Latin American and Caribbean countries will discuss the framework, strategies and prospects of international energy policy cooperation.
