Foreign Minister Westerwelle on the need to strongly protect freedom of opinion and the media - Federal Foreign Office 
    30.12.2010 - Press release

“The abortive attack in Copenhagen shows how important it is to strongly protect freedom of opinion and freedom of the media. In a democracy the right to express opinions freely is as vital as the air we breathe. We must not let extremists undermine the freedom that is fundamental to our thinking and way of life.”
